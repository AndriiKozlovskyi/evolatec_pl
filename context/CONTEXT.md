# Project context

## Business
Name: [your agency name — e.g. WebTech PL or your chosen brand]
Location: Poland (primary market — Warsaw, Kraków, Wrocław, Trójmiasto)
Niche: Web agency — professional websites, SEO, online marketing for Polish businesses

## Target buyer
**Who:** Small and medium business owners in Poland — local service providers, 
startups, tradespeople, clinics, law firms, real estate agencies, e-commerce operators.
**What they want:** A professional website that looks credible, loads fast, 
and actually brings in customers — not just a "business card" site.
**What they fear:**
- Paying a lot for something that sits unused and invisible in Google
- Being locked into a WordPress site that breaks every 3 months
- Not knowing what they're paying for or how long it will take
- Getting something generic that looks like every other competitor

## Tone
Direct, expert, zero fluff. Like a senior developer explaining options to a 
smart client — confident and specific, never salesy. No exclamation marks. 
No "we are passionate about". State facts, let the reader draw conclusions.
Polish-language copy should feel like it was written by a native Polish tech professional,
not translated from German or English.

## USPs — specific and verifiable
1. Delivery from 3 working days for a landing page — not weeks of waiting
2. Lighthouse score 95–100/100 — measurable, not claimed
3. Load time under 1 second — vs 3–8 seconds typical for WordPress
4. GEO optimization included in every project at no extra cost — 
   visibility in ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity via Schema.org markup
5. Fixed-price quotes — no scope creep surprises
6. No WordPress, no plugins — custom Vue.js / Nuxt stack means 
   no security holes, no plugin updates, no crashes

## Tech stack (for copy reference)
